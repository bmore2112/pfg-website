Our Story

The story behind Premiumfangirls.

I founded Premiumfangirls in 2022, and since then we have helped hundreds of creators and agencies build real, lasting businesses in this industry.

My own path started in front of the camera. I was a content creator myself, I went viral more than once, and I always had a genuine obsession with social media and the way attention actually moves online. That instinct became the foundation for everything we do today.

In 2022, a friend of mine started working on OnlyFans and asked for help running her accounts. What began as a favor quickly turned into something much bigger. I saw a real opportunity to turn creator management into an actual business, one that could give more creators the structure, marketing, and production they were missing.

Over the last four years I have also spent a large part of my time coaching and teaching other agency owners, helping them build stronger companies and genuinely improve their creators' lives through smarter monetization.

I have seen the darker side of this industry too, the same way many of you have. That is exactly why my most important principle has never changed. Give creators a great experience at a fair and reasonable percentage. No games, no fine print, no disappearing act.

Today I lead a full team and work with a roster of serious, high-earning creators. But the goal has always been simple, and it is the same one you have as you read this. Make money, and become free.
